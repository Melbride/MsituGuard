About MsituGuard

MsituGuard is Kenya's leading environmental protection platform connecting forest-adjacent communities with local organizations to monitor, report, and address environmental threats.

We believe in community-driven conservation. By empowering citizens to report environmental issues and connecting them with verified organizations, we protect Kenya's precious ecosystems together.

Platform Features

Comprehensive tools designed for environmental protection and community conservation

Environmental Reports

Report environmental threats like illegal logging, pollution, and wildlife poaching. Get real-time updates on report status from verified organizations.

Community Forums

Connect with fellow environmental guardians, share conservation tips, discuss local environmental issues, and build stronger community networks.

15 Billion Trees Initiative

Participate in Kenya's ambitious tree planting program. Document your tree planting activities and contribute to national environmental goals.

Smart Notifications

Get instant notifications when your reports are verified or resolved. Stay updated on environmental issues in your area through email and in-app alerts.

Automatically capture precise locations for environmental reports. GPS integration ensures accurate mapping of environmental threats and conservation efforts.

Fire Safety Center

Assess fire risk in your area using real-time weather data. Report fire observations to protect Kenya's forests from wildfire threats.

Impact Tracking

Track your environmental contributions with personal dashboards. See how your reports and tree planting activities make a real difference in conservation.
